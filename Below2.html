Gameplay

Buckshot Roulette (Also known as ลูกตะกั่วคมๆ from the developer team.) a computer game made by Mike Klubnika.

The game started with the menu above, after the players press "Play" the game will send the player to the bathroom, after that the player needs to go to balcony above the club to go to The Dealer's office.

After that The Dealer will appear with the messages "Please sign the waiver", the player must sign their name.

Then, after sign the waiver the game instantly start without tutorial.

The game basically the game is to grab a shotgun type 12-gauge and shoot yourself with a shotgun or shoot The Dealer, if the bullet is real (Live rounds) the player/Dealer will take the damage, if the bullet is fake (Blanks) nobody will take the damage.

The first round Player and The Dealer start with a 2 charge.

The second round Player and The Dealer start with a 4 charge, this round is the first round that includes items.

Lastly, the third round Player and The Dealer start with total of 6 charge. 4 normal charge and 2 last charge (Note: This wasn't an official name.)

After Player/The Dealer having 2 charges left they will enter Last Chance (Note: This wasn't an official name either.)

Meaning if they got lives round on them they will instantly die.

If could beat The Dealer, The Dealer will die and reward you with money around 70K dollars. (The money will be less up to ML. of beer drink, blanks/lives shotted, and cigarettes used.)
